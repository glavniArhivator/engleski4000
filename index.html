4000 Essential English Words

Interactive Vocabulary Learning Tool

Unit 1

agree
to have the same opinion or belief as another person
The students agree they have too much homework.
alcohol
a type of drink that can make people drunk
A person should not drive a car after he or she has been drinking alcohol.
arrive
to get somewhere
They arrived at school at 7 a.m.
August
the eighth month of the year
Is your birthday in August?
boat
a vehicle that moves across water
We will take a boat to cross the river.

Story: A Day at the Beach

Last August, my friends and I went to the beach. We arrived early in the morning and rented a boat. We all agreed it would be a fun day. Nobody brought alcohol because we wanted to stay safe on the water. It was a perfect summer day!

Unit 2

basic
simple or fundamental; the most important part of something
Learning the basic rules is the first step to playing any game.
become
to start to be something
She wants to become a doctor when she grows up.
capture
to catch or take control of something
The photographer tried to capture the perfect sunset.
challenge
something difficult that tests your ability
Climbing the mountain was a real challenge for the team.
chapter
one part or section of a book
I read three chapters of my novel last night.
